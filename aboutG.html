About Guruji

GURUJI SHRI RISHI PRABHAKAR

Guruji Rishi Prabhakar was most importantly, a builder of world leaders. He was the founder of the extremely powerful Siddha Samadhi Yoga (SSY) Programme. He is aworld-renowned teacher of meditation, self-management and social consciousness. He was a computer scientist and aeronauticalengineer, and a management graduate from Western Ontario University, Canada.
A silent meditator, he was an active inspiration through his simplicity and dynamism for tens of thousands around the world whose lives have been touched by him and his dedicated band of leaders.

Pujya Guruji’s discourses, programmes and trainings still keep having a permanent impact on people’s lives and open their hearts to greater joyful possibilities that are normally unavailable.

Guruji attained Mahasamadhi at Bangalore on Feb 16, 2014 at the age of 66. He is survived by his mother Ramadevi Amma, his wife Arundhati and son Siddhant who have imbibed his spirit and together with other teachers and disciples now stand committed to build his dream world through the organisation’s 600 centres in India and abroad..

Some Facts About Guruji

  • Rishi Prabhakar was an Indian Spiritualist and Philosophiser who is known to develop Siddha Samadhi Yoga (SSY). His other programs include Kaya Kalpa Kriya (KKK), Rapid Comprehension and Review Technique (RCRT), Bhava Samadhi Training (BST), Advanced Meditation Course (AMC), Infant Siddha Program (ISP), and Inspirational Leadership in Rural Development (ILRD).

  • Rishi was brought up by his grandparents. He grew up fatherless and was acquainted with the fact around the age of 7 or 8. At the same time, he also came to know that Ramadevi was his mother and not his sister as he was made to believe

  • Prabhakar was intelligent, obedient, and caring as a child who always stood up for his principles. He was inspired by the books of Swami Vivekananda.
